How to Make it

Step 1

Preheat oven to 450°

Step 2

Place potatoes in a medium saucepan; cover with water. Bring to a boil and immer 12 minutes or until tender.Drain.

Step 3

Heat a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat. Add 1 teaspoon oil to pan. Sprinkle chicken whith 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper. And chicken and thyme springs to pan; cook 5 minutes or until chicken is browned. Turn chicken over. Place pan in oven; bake at 450° for 10 minutes or until chicken is done. Remove chicken from pan.

Step 4

Return pan to medium high heat. Add remaining 2 teaspoons oil. Add potatoes, cut sides down; mushrooms; and 1 table spoon thyme; cook 3 minutes or until browned, stirring once.
